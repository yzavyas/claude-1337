# Bibliography

Primary sources for claims in the documentation.

## Productivity

- METR (2025). Measuring the Impact of Early AI Assistance. RCT, n=16 experienced developers on mature codebases.
- Stack Overflow Developer Survey (2024, 2025). Annual surveys on developer tools and practices.
- Fastly (2025). AI Code Generation Survey. n=791 developers, verification time by seniority.

## Cognitive Effects

- Lee, H.P. et al. (2025). The Impact of Generative AI on Critical Thinking. CHI 2025. Cross-sectional, β = -0.69.
- Kosmyna, N. et al. (2025). AI-Assisted Writing and Memory Retention. MIT Media Lab.
- Bastani, H. et al. (2025). Generative AI Can Harm Learning. PNAS. Scaffolded vs unrestricted AI access.
- Bansal, G. et al. (2021). Does the Whole Exceed its Parts? The Effect of AI Explanations. CHI 2021.

## Skill Degradation

- Budzyń, B. et al. (2025). Effect of AI-Assisted Colonoscopy on Adenoma Detection. Lancet Gastroenterology & Hepatology. Crossover RCT, 3-month follow-up.
- GitClear (2024). Coding on Copilot: 2024 Data Suggests Downward Pressure on Code Quality. 211M lines analyzed.

## Collaboration Design

- Blaurock, M. et al. (2024). AI-Based Service Experience Contingencies. Journal of Service Research. Meta-analysis of 106 studies.

## Trust and Verification

- DORA Report (2024). Accelerate State of DevOps. Google Cloud.
- Veracode (2025). State of Software Security: GenAI Impact Report.
